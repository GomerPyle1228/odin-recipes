Wiener Schnitzel

Finished Wiener Schnitzel

Translation of the name: 'Wiener' this word comes from the word 'Wien', which is the Austrian city called Vienna. 'Schnitzel' means basically meat in a crust. I'm German and hope you can understand my English description. Serve the schnitzels with salad, ketchup and French fries.

Ingredients

  • 2 lb veal
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • Salt and Pepper
  • 4 cups bread crumbs
  • 1/8 cup of oil

Steps

  1. Cut the veal into steaks, about as thick as a finger.
  2. Dredge in flour.
  3. In a shallow dish, beat the eggs with 1 tablespoon oil, salt and pepper.
  4. Coat the veal with egg mixture, then with bread crumbs.
  5. Heat 1/4 cup oil in a heavy skillet over medium heat.
  6. Fry veal until golden brown, about 5 minutes on each side.
